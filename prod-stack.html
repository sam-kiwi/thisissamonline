  • Productivity Stack

I have a lot of opinions surrounding productivity - in a nutshell being "productive" as measured by economic output is not a measure of our worth in society.

That being said, here is a list of tools that help my work life run a little bit smoother. While these don't necessarily boost my productivity in a measurable way, they are products that allow me to go about my day with less frustration than I would otherwise experience. Needless to say, almost all of these products provides a delightful user experience.

  • Note Taking - Notion
  • To-Do - Notion (I've created a personal Kanban board within Notion to track my daily, weekly, and longer-term to-do items)
  • Email - Superhuman (Yes, it's $30/month. And yes, it's worth it.)
  • Content Search - Command E
  • Text Editing - Sublime Text (or Vim within Command Line)
